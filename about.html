Our Story

Passionate about Lagos flavours, community, and coastal ingredients.

Our Lagos Journey

From a neighbourhood kitchen to a celebrated Lagos dining destination.

2012

The Beginning

Akinnukawe Israel opened a small coastal kitchen focused on fresh seafood and traditional Nigerian flavours with a modern touch.

2016

Local Recognition

Our dedication to Lagos cuisine earned local awards and a devoted following across the city.

2019

Expansion

We moved to our flagship Victoria Island location, expanded our menu, and launched private dining experiences.

2026

Today & Beyond

Food continues to celebrate local producers while innovating traditional recipes for new audiences.
