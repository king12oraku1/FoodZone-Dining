Reservation Policy

We recommend booking at least two weeks in advance for dinner reservations and one month for private events. A credit card hold is required for parties of 6 or more.

Cancellations must be made 24 hours prior to your reservation to avoid a 10,000 NGN per person fee.

Dress Code

Smart elegant attire is required. Gentlemen are encouraged to wear collared shirts. Sportswear, shorts, and flip-flops are not permitted.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions? ▼
Absolutely. We offer vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and allergen-conscious menus. Please inform us of any dietary needs when making your reservation so our kitchen can prepare accordingly.
Is there parking available? ▼
Valet parking is available Thursday through Sunday. There is also a public parking garage located opposite the restaurant. Street parking is limited.
Can I host a private event on a Sunday? ▼
Yes, the Sapphire Room is available for private events seven days a week. Sunday events benefit from reduced minimums and flexible timing. Contact our events team for a custom quote.
Do you offer gift certificates? ▼
Yes, gift certificates are available in denominations from $100 to $1,000. They can be purchased in-person or over the phone and are valid for one year from the date of issue.
What is your corkage policy? ▼
We allow one bottle per couple with a corkage fee of 50000N` per bottle. The wine must not be featured on our current list. Large format bottles are subject to different pricing.
